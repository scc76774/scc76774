Activities

As a babysitter, I believe it is my job to get kids away from the TV and off electronics. Typically when I babysit, we play board games, go outside and play sports, have dance parties, play pretend, and have singing contests. Also, with the parents premission of course, we have gone to the pool, gone to an amusement park, went to the movies, went to get ice cream/slurpies, and gone to the park. I always ask the parents what they are comfortable with their kids doing before I do anything. I have my own car, so I can drive the kids anywhere. But sometimes the most fun is found right at home! Also, I know it's not all fun and games. I am more than happy to help your kids do their homework and make sure they go to bed on time.
